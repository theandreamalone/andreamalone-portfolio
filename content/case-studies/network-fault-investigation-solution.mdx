I made the investigation path legible by connecting alert summaries, impacted-site views, KPI trends, router and circuit evidence, ticket status, and responder actions in one continuous flow rather than leaving users to reconstruct the sequence across separate tabs. I used consistent overview, list, map, performance, and ticket patterns so responders could move from a large outage or cell alert into the affected sites, review supporting network data, and decide whether to call the NOC, generate a script, or review and amend a ticket without losing context.

Severity and priority were communicated through consistent labels, color coding, and status treatments, while evidence remained visible through KPI summaries, charts, site details, map and list views, and linked ticket information. The system surfaced evidence and options; responders retained the call.
