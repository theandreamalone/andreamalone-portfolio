Before Network Fault Investigation Dashboard, field responders worked across specialized screens and tabs within one system for RAN outage analysis, site monitoring, router-port investigation, AAV circuits, BFD alerts, and impacted sites. Related evidence was distributed across dense tables, charts, maps, and ticket views, requiring users to switch contexts and repeatedly reorient themselves as they moved from detection to diagnosis. Because these users were consuming operational information rather than entering data, the problem was not manual input; it was maintaining a clear investigation path across fragmented views. The redesign connected outage summaries, performance evidence, impacted-site views, tickets, and responder actions into a more continuous workflow.
