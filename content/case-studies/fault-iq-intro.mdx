I worked on Fault IQ, a mobile network-operations module that helps responders investigate current cell alerts and large outages across RAN, SIP, BFD, and vendor-circuit conditions. The workflow moves from alert selection into impacted sites, diagnostics, KPI and performance views, ticket review or amendment, and responder actions such as calling the NOC or generating a script. I treated it as an incident-analysis path rather than a collection of dashboards, keeping impact, evidence, and escalation actions connected as users moved from detection toward response.
