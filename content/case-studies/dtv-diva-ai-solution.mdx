I made the Thread the primary interaction surface, with the Home composer initiating a conversation and each response preserving the evidence required for human judgment through View Sources, View Table, and View Response Time controls, reinforced by a visible Thinking state that communicated system activity without implying certainty; the assistant returned and contextualized information directly, while users remained responsible for judging whether its evidence supported a business decision, making verifiability the trust layer rather than adding an invented escalation workflow; Admin and Tools surfaces supported scope, configuration, and usage oversight without competing with the core exchange, and I delivered the shipped product at specification fidelity across Mobile, 1056, and 1312 breakpoints with responsive rules, states, tokens, redlines, and QA handoff documentation.
