## Why It's Different

Most "AI portfolio" tools work like a chatbot: ask it anything, and it generates an answer on the fly. That's flexible, but it means the text you read was invented in the moment — no one reviewed it, and it can drift from what actually happened.

This site works differently. Every case study is written by me, in advance, reviewed for accuracy. The AI's only job is to route you to the right one. Nothing you read here was improvised — it's curated.
