NetHive IQ was a 54-screen enterprise network operations platform for a Fortune 100 telecommunications company, spanning independent sub-apps for fault analysis, executive monitoring, and AI-assisted incident support. As the platform expanded across independently designed sub-apps, the team needed to understand whether the experience still worked as one connected system rather than a collection of separate products. I approached the work as a platform-level systems problem, looking beyond individual screens to understand how navigation, interaction patterns, and shared components could create a more coherent operational experience.
