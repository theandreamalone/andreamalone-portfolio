I led the experience design for an AI assistant embedded within a broader network operations intelligence platform for a Fortune 100 telecommunications company. It brought together information otherwise scattered across the platform, then surfaced it through a single conversational interface. Network operations responders and executives used it to ask questions about network health, outages, and active incidents while coordinating under time pressure. My scope covered the assistant's end-to-end experience across mobile and desktop web apps, including how it delivered situation-aware guidance without adding another disconnected tool to an already demanding operational environment.
