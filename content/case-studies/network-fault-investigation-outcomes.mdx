Network Fault Investigation Dashboard reached a client-approved, development-ready state across the core investigation flows, including outage analysis, impacted sites, performance views, ticket handling, and responder actions. The work established reusable patterns for list and map views, KPI summaries, filters, ticket states, and script-generation paths while preserving the exceptions required by specific fault types. The result was a clearer operational structure for engineering to implement and a more continuous investigation path for field responders, without claiming unverified efficiency or adoption gains.
