## What the AI is actually doing

There are three layers to this, and they're not equally important.

**1. Understanding what you're asking.** Turning a real question into what you're actually trying to learn. This is the one layer that genuinely requires AI — a dropdown or a set of filter chips can't do this part.

**2. Deciding which evidence answers it.** (This is where almost all the value lives.) Not which template to show — which specific projects, artifacts, and metrics answer your specific question. Two people asking about "leadership" might see entirely different case studies, because they're not really asking the same thing.

**3. Arranging it.** Ordering, emphasis, what's foregrounded versus available-if-you-want-it. This is polish — real, but it's the smallest of the three layers.
