## What the AI never does

It never writes. Every word of prose on this site — every case study, every claim, every metric — was written by me, ahead of time, and lives in version-controlled files the AI can select from but cannot edit or generate. The AI's role stops at choosing and arranging what already exists. It doesn't improvise a new sentence about my work, ever, for anyone.

That's a deliberate constraint, not a limitation I'm working around. A recruiter or hiring manager reading this site is forming a judgment about my work — the one place that judgment can't afford to be wrong is in the words themselves. So the AI doesn't touch them.
