## The problem with a normal portfolio

A traditional portfolio is one fixed document. Every visitor scrolls the same case studies in the same order, regardless of what they came here to find out. If you're a recruiter trying to answer "has she led teams at scale?" — you have to hunt for it, buried between projects that don't answer your question at all.

This site works differently: the page becomes the answer to your actual question, built from real evidence, at the moment you ask.

Ask something like "has she done human-in-the-loop AI work?" and the page rebuilds around the projects, artifacts, and outcomes that actually answer that — not a generic "AI experience" section that sort of, kind of, gets there.
