An AI router composes this interface by selecting which evidence answers your question and how to arrange it. It returns content IDs only. It cannot write, edit, or generate a sentence; all prose is authored by me and version-controlled. The only generated text on this site is a bounded summary of that pre-authored metadata, slotted into authored templates — it restates your question and points to the evidence, but it never writes the narrative itself. In AI product design, deciding where generation stops is the design decision. Full case studies remain one click away.
